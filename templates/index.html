What is SlideShare?

SlideShare is an online platform where people can share and discover presentations, documents, infographics, and videos. It's owned by LinkedIn, part of Microsoft. It was first launched in 2006 and later acquired by LinkedIn in 2012.

Think of it as a "YouTube for slides" but instead of videos, you can explore PowerPoint presentations, PDFs, Keynote files, and professional reports uploaded by users around the world.
